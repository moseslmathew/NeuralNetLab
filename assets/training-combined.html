The Learning Cycle

From Theory to Reality

⚽

01. Takes Action

The robot observes the environment and uses its current weights to make a move — without knowing if it's correct.

📡

02. Gets Feedback

A "Refereee" (Reward Function) judges the action. Success grants positive points, failure gives a penalty.

🔗

03. Adjusts Connections

Backpropagation pushes the feedback through the network, "rewiring" the weights to favor success.

🏆

04. Evolves Strategy

Repeating this loop millions of times turns random noise into complex, winning behaviors.
