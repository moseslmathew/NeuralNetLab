How the Robot Learns

🤖 No human is teaching the robot! The game has a built-in "referee" — simple rules coded in advance that automatically judge each action:

  • Score a goal → +3.0 points
  • Shot on target → +0.8 points
  • Trick kick works → +1.0 points
  • Concede a goal → −2.5 points
  • Shot goes wide → −0.4 points

The robot tries random actions, sees whether the score goes up or down, and gradually figures out what works. This is called Reinforcement Learning — learning by trial and error, just like a child learning to ride a bike.
